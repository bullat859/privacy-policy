Privacy Policy: Dynamic Edge Gesture Control

Last Updated: October 2023

Dynamic Edge Gesture Control ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data within our Android utility application. By using Dynamic Edge Gesture Control, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Permissions and Data Collection

To provide advanced gesture controls and media features, the app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera and Microphone: Required to enable "Silent Photo," "Silent Video," and "Silent Audio Recording" features.
  • Foreground Services: Used to allow media capture to continue while you use other applications.
  • Notifications: Required to provide transparent alerts regarding active recording sessions.
  • Notification Policy Access: Used to allow you to toggle your device's sound modes (Normal, Vibrate, Silent) via gestures.

2. Transparency and Foreground Services

We believe in full transparency. When you initiate a background audio or video recording, the app triggers a mandatory, visible notification. This notification serves to inform you that a recording is in progress and provides an easy, one-tap "Stop Recording" button to cease the activity immediately.

3. Data Storage and Handling

All data is stored locally. Dynamic Edge Gesture Control uses the Android Storage Access Framework to save your photos, videos, and audio recordings directly to your device's local storage (such as Pictures/EdgeControl, Movies/EdgeControl, or Music/EdgeControl folders).

We do not collect, transmit, upload, or share your personal data, media, contacts, or usage history with any external servers or third-party entities. All processing and storage remain entirely on your device.

4. Third-Party Interactions

The app interacts with your device's system settings to perform tasks configured by your gestures, such as toggling the flashlight or adjusting screen timeout settings. The app may launch web searches or open specific files/contacts based on your custom configuration. These actions are triggered locally by your explicit input.

5. Compliance with Policies

This application strictly adheres to Google Play Developer Policies regarding User Data, Permissions, and Device Abuse. We do not engage in unauthorized background data collection or deceptive behaviors.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer support channels provided in the Google Play Store listing.
