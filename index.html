Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dynamic Edge Gesture Control handles information when you use our application. We are committed to your privacy and transparency.

1. Data Collection & Processing

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

Dynamic Edge Gesture Control is a local utility application. All operations, including gesture processing and media capturing, occur strictly on your device.

2. App Permissions & Functionality

To provide advanced functionality, our app requests specific permissions. These are used locally and for the following purposes:

  • Camera & Microphone: Required to enable "Silent Photo," "Silent Video," and "Silent Audio Recording" features.
  • Foreground Services: Used to ensure background recordings continue while you use other apps. A mandatory, visible notification is displayed while these services are active to ensure transparency.
  • Notification Permission: Required to display recording status alerts and system notifications.
  • Notification Policy Access: Used to toggle your device’s sound modes (Normal, Vibrate, Silent) based on your custom gestures.

3. Local Storage of Media

When you use the app to capture media (photos, videos, or audio), those files are saved only to your device's local storage. They are stored in app-private directories or standard system folders (such as Pictures, Movies, or Music). We do not have access to these files, and we do not upload them to any cloud or remote server.

4. Third-Party Interactions

The app does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising SDKs. It may trigger system intents (like opening a browser or your contacts) based on gestures you explicitly configure, but these actions are performed solely by your device's own system applications.

5. Compliance with Policies

We comply with all Google Play Developer Policies regarding User Data and Permissions. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not share, sell, or disclose information to third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
