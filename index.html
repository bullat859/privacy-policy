Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy governs the privacy practices of the mobile application Dynamic Edge Gesture Control (referred to as "the Application", "We", "Our", or "Us"). The Application provides customizable edge controls, gesture interactions, and system overlay utility features to enhance user accessibility and experience.

1. Information Collection and Use

We are deeply committed to protecting user privacy. The Application is designed to process data locally on your device. Unless explicitly stated below, personal information is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Sensitive Device Permissions

To provide advanced edge gesture detection and automation services, the Application requests access to specific high-risk device capabilities. These permissions are used strictly for local feature execution:

  • System Overlay (Draw Over Other Apps): Required to maintain a custom overlay that displays interactive edge handles over mobile settings, status bars, and the notch area to capture user edge swipes.
  • Camera & Microphone Permissions: Used explicitly to execute the optional, user-configured silent media capture features (taking a background photo, recording silent audio, or capturing silent video) entirely locally.
  • Notification Access (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): Required on Android 13+ to post a visible foreground service status notification when background gesture listeners or media utilities are actively processing data.
Important Data Transparency Notice: All captured media (photos, videos, or audio recordings) triggered via edge gesture commands are stored directly inside your device's storage (public MediaStore galleries or app-private storage directories) based on your chosen settings. This data is handled entirely offline and is never uploaded, transferred, or shared by us.

3. Third-Party Services & SDKs

The Application may incorporate external software development kits (SDKs) to maintain technical stability, perform error analytics, or execute specific app actions (such as split-screen integrations or freeform windows). We evaluate these dependencies to ensure their privacy standards strictly respect data boundaries.

4. Data Deletion and Control

Because the Application stores configuration data locally on your system hardware, you hold full control over your information. You can instantly erase all custom gesture data, edge mappings, and application logs by clearing the Application storage cache via your Android System Settings or completely uninstalling the application.

5. Compliance with Developer Policies

This privacy framework is rigorously maintained to comply with current marketplace guidelines, including the Google Play Developer Program Policies regarding Privacy, Deception, Device Abuse, and User Data handling transparency.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any modifications. Changes are effective immediately upon being published to this URL destination.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, feedback, or data transparency inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy or the operation of Dynamic Edge Gesture Control, please contact the developer via your designated support channels.
